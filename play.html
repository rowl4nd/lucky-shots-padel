The courts

Padel the way it should be — properly built and always available.

Four full-size indoor courts built to a proper standard, not retrofitted into a space that wasn't designed for them. Panoramic glass walls, consistent court surfaces and full lighting so evening and weekend play look exactly the same as midweek mornings. Covered from the weather, open from day one.

What you get
01Four courts

Enough to run multiple sessions, social events and a league simultaneously — not a single court squeezed into a corner. Four full-size courts, properly spaced, with room to play properly.

02Panoramic glass

Full glass walls on every court. Watch from the bar, from the sideline, from wherever. The glass doesn't just look right — it makes the courts feel bigger and keeps the social connection between play and the rest of the club.

03All-weather, all year

Indoor means indoor. No cancellations because it's blowing a gale off the Conwy Estuary, no boggy surfaces, no seasonal closure. Year-round play, regardless of what's happening outside.

04Evening lighting

Fully lit for after-work, weekend evenings and winter afternoons. You're not squinting at a ball against a dark background — consistent lighting across all four courts, all hours.

05Professional surface

The court surface matters. Consistent, purpose-built padel surfaces across all four courts — the same underfoot every time, which makes a real difference when you're learning or competing.

All levels, genuinely

Whether you've never held a racket or play competitively.

Beginner

Start here.
