What we're building

A proper bar and café that earns its place — not an afterthought, not a vending machine.

Most sports venues treat food and drink as a box to tick. Lucky Shots is built the other way around — the Social side is designed from the start as somewhere people actually want to be. Somewhere to debrief the last point, to celebrate, to linger. Somewhere you'd come without a racket.

The atmosphere

Designed for staying, not just visiting.

The space is being designed around the way people actually use a club — not just arriving and leaving, but arriving early, staying late, and coming back the next week. Good light, good seats, and a bar that doesn't feel like it was fitted out at a trade counter.

We're working with local producers and makers across everything from the coffee on the counter to the beer on tap. Partners announced as soon as we're ready.
