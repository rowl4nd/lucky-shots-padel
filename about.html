OUR STORY

Built for North Wales.

Lucky Shots Padel is being built from scratch in Colwyn Bay — a proper indoor padel facility designed to be a real part of the community.

Four courts, a social space worth spending time in, and a coaching programme that starts where you are. We wanted somewhere that felt designed, not just built. Somewhere with local identity. Somewhere as good after the game as it is on the court.
