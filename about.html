About

An independent padel club for North Wales.

What

Four indoor panoramic courts, a bar and café, coaching and events — all under one roof. Built for the area, open to everyone.

When

Opening October 2026. Founding membership and early booking available to the list ahead of launch.

Who

Independent. Not a franchise, not a chain. Lucky Shots is built specifically for North Wales — locally run, long term.
