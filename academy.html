03 — ACADEMY

Whatever your level.

Whether you've never held a racket or you're ready to compete, the Lucky Shots Academy will have a programme for you. Term-based timetables, structured progression, and a head coach joining the team ahead of opening.

THE APPROACH

Community first, competition second.

The Lucky Shots Academy is built around the idea that coaching should do more than improve technique — it should build a playing community. Saturday mornings, after-school sessions, ladies evenings. Scheduled to fit real life.

Our head coach will be confirmed ahead of opening. If you're a coach interested in joining the team, get in touch.
