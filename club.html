The soul of Lucky Shots.

This isn't a sports hall with a vending machine. The social arm is where the brand lives — good coffee, cold beer, fresh bakes, and an atmosphere that makes the post-match hour the best part of your day. Designed to be a destination first, sports facility second.

THE EXPERIENCE

Play. Eat.
Stay longer.

Court-side seating, the bar within reach, and good people to share it with. Lucky Shots is being designed as a destination — not just a facility. Come for the sport. Stay for everything else.

WHAT TO EXPECT

More than a post-match drink.

The Lucky Shots social space is being designed with the same care as the courts. Considered interiors, proper coffee, good beer, food worth eating — and enough room to actually sit down together after a game.

We'll also stock a small range of padel equipment and apparel. The kind of place you can kit out and fuel up in one stop.
